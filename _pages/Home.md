### About me

I am a Member of Technical Staff on Anthropic's Economic Research team, where I study the economic impacts of AI. Previously, I was a Research Scientist at Meta's Economics and Policy Research Team, where I led economic impact measurement at scale, and a Postdoctoral Scholar at the [Golub Capital Social Impact Lab](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/faculty-research/labs-initiatives/sil) at Stanford GSB. I received my PhD in Economics from [Columbia University](https://econ.columbia.edu/) in 2023.

My research develops econometric methods for causal inference and structural estimation with data generated by AI and machine learning, drawing on Bayesian inference and probabilistic programming, with applications to labor markets, health, and platform economics.
